The Golden Boot is won by the player who scores the most goals in a single season in the Premier League.

The Golden Boot is an extremely impressive achievement for any striker in the Premier League, especially considering the quality of the defending team's players and goalkeepers. Many strive to score 20+ goals per season, however the Golden Boot winners usually score 30+ in a season.
